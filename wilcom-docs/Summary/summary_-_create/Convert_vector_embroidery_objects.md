EmbroideryStudio lets you convert vector graphics directly to embroidery objects using a variety of techniques. Similarly, you can convert embroidery designs or objects to vector graphics. You can also convert between different [Run](../../glossary/glossary) and [Column C](../../glossary/glossary) objects.
